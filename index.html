Our Menu

Nature Chef's Choice

Traditional Food

Embark on a culinary journey with our Traditional Food selection, crafted with authentic recipes and fresh ingredients. From mouthwatering curries to fragrant rice dishes, experience the rich flavors of tradition in every bite.

Special Indian Bread

Thandori Roti

Indulge in the aromatic flavors of our Thandori Roti, a specialty bread cooked in a traditional tandoor oven. Served hot and fresh, it's the perfect accompaniment to your favorite curry or dish.

Special Indian Cuisine

Hyderabadi Biryani

Indulge in the aromatic flavors of our Hyderabadi Biryani, a classic Indian dish made with basmati rice, tender meat, and a blend of spices. Served with raita and salan, it's a culinary delight that will transport you to the streets of Hyderabad.

Special South Indian Delight

Dosa Delight

Indulge in our delicious Dosa's,, a South Indian specialty made from fermented rice and lentil batter, cooked to perfection on a hot griddle. Served with flavorful chutneys and sambar, it's a breakfast delight you won't want to miss!

Special Breakfast Delight

Omelet Bread

Start your day right with our Bread Omelet Specials. Made with fluffy omelet stuffed between slices of toasted bread and served with a side of crispy hash browns, it's a hearty and delicious breakfast that will keep you energized throughout the day.

Special Indian Sweet

Jilebi

Indulge in the irresistible sweetness of our Jilebi, a popular Indian dessert made from deep-fried batter soaked in sugar syrup. Crispy on the outside and juicy on the inside, it's a delightful treat for your taste buds!

Fish Special Dish

Fish Delight

Enjoy our Fish Delight, made with fresh catch of the day, seasoned with aromatic herbs and spices, and grilled to perfection. Served with a side of vegetables, it's a culinary experience that will tantalize your taste buds.

Special Healthy Option

Raw Nutrition Food

Discover the goodness of Raw Nutrition Food, packed with vitamins, minerals, and essential nutrients. Our carefully curated selection of raw dishes offers a wholesome and nourishing option for those seeking a nutritious diet.

Special Healthy Snack

Fruity Delight

Introduce your child to a delightful mix of fresh strawberries, juicy black grapes, and sweet cherries. Packed with vitamins and antioxidants, it's the perfect start to their day!

Special Indian Snak

Samosa

Indulge in the crispy goodness of our Samosa, a popular Indian snack filled with spicy potatoes and peas, wrapped in a crunchy pastry shell. Served with tangy chutneys, it's the perfect evening treat to satisfy your cravings.

Special Delightful Treat

Cup Cakes

Indulge in our delectable Cup Cakes, baked to perfection with premium ingredients and topped with rich frosting. Perfect for any occasion or as a delightful treat for yourself.

Special Street Food Delight

Pani Puri

Savor the burst of flavors with our Pani Puri, a popular Indian street food delicacy. Crisp puris filled with spicy potato masala and tangy tamarind water, it's a refreshing and delightful snack to enjoy any time of the day.

Special Asian Cuisine

Noodles Delight

Satisfy your cravings with our Noodles Delight, a delectable dish inspired by Asian cuisine. Made with stir-fried noodles, fresh vegetables, and your choice of protein, it's a flavorful and comforting meal that will leave you wanting more.

Special Italian Classic

Pizza Supreme

Savor the flavors of our Pizza Supreme, loaded with a variety of toppings including pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, olives, and more. Baked to perfection, it's a taste sensation you won't want to miss!
